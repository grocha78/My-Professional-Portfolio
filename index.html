About Me

Guadalupe Rocha is a Houston-based technical, musical and visual project. Intricately creating sonic soundscapes on a nightly basis with homemade synths and other electronics, he hopes to bring his listeners into total awareness of sound and the musical satori that reveals itself to him on these late, fateful nights.Decimating the dividing line between sound and vision, Mr. Rocha envisions an all encompassing sensory experience, where performer and listener are arrayed like ever-shifting plot points on an as-yet uncharted sonic grid.

As he deftly uses his handmade controller, he takes us on a sweep from clustered buzz-toothed sine waves into lulliing hypnotic bass perched at the threshold of the audible and physical. In the world of electronic experimentation, there is not yet a name for what Guadalupe Rocha is creating. 'When the mode of the music changes, the walls of the city shake.'
